How to Use

  1. Click "Start Camera" and allow camera access
  2. Position yourself so your full body is visible
  3. Raise your hands above your shoulders - timer starts automatically with a BEEP
  4. Hold the position as long as you can
  5. When you drop down, timer stops with a BEEP and takes a snapshot
  6. Your best time will be saved until you reset

Tip: Make sure you're serving this page via HTTP (not file://) for camera access to work!
